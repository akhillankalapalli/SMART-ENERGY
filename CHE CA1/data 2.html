Global Market Growth and Adoption

The global smart grid market is expected to grow from $33.9 billion in 2022 to $66.7 billion by 2030 with a CAGR of 8.7%. This growth is driven by renewable energy integration and smart grid technologies.

Renewable Energy Integration

Renewable energy now accounts for 38% of global electricity generation, with projections to rise to 50% by 2030. Smart grids and storage solutions are key to efficiently integrating renewables into the grid.

AI in Energy

AI-driven technologies are revolutionizing energy management, helping utilities predict demand, optimize grid operations, and reduce inefficiencies. The market for AI in energy is expected to grow from $2.1 billion in 2022 to $12.6 billion by 2030.

Electric Vehicle Integration

Electric vehicles (EVs) are accelerating in popularity, with EV sales expected to make up 40% of total car sales by 2030. Smart charging networks and V2G technology are vital to ensuring the grid remains stable while meeting the growing demand.

Energy Efficiency and Sustainability

Energy efficiency technologies and decarbonization strategies are crucial for reducing emissions. The adoption of energy-efficient solutions like smart meters and AI-powered energy management is expected to help achieve global sustainability goals.
